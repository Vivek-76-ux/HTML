Biography

Linus Torvalds is a Finnish-American software engineer who is the creator and principal developer of the Linux kernel, which is the kernel for a number of operating systems. He also created the version control system Git. ;Torvlads was born on December 28,1969 in Helsinki, Finland. He studied computer science at the University of Helsinki, and he developed the Linux kernel as a student project in 1991. Since then,the linux kernel has become one of the most widely used kernels in the world.
